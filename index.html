A Call Towards Sustainibility

Amidst this pandemic, we all have read many articles stating the carbon emission has lowered by 17 percent, seen photos of clear waters in Venice canal, clear Delhi skies. Many animals returned to their indigenous habitats where humans took over. But recently National Geographic has published an article where it has stated that these changes will be temporary but rather after this pandemic is over we will see an increased amount of pollution levels. And this might be true, as a recent study shows that the carbon emissions are only 5 percent lower than at the same point in 2019, even though normal activity has not yet fully restarted.
